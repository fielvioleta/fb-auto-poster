Privacy Policy

Daily Wings & Cafe Auto-Poster · Last updated: July 3, 2026

This application ("the App") is an internal social media publishing tool used by Daily Wings & Cafe to automatically create and publish posts to its own Facebook Page. It is not a consumer-facing product and does not offer login, accounts, or services to the general public.

Information We Collect

The App accesses and processes only the following, all belonging to our own Facebook Page:

  • The Page's basic profile information (Page ID and name).
  • Photos and posts previously published on the Page, used to select images for new posts.
  • A Page access token, used solely to publish posts on the Page's behalf.

The App does not collect, store, or process any personal data from Facebook users, Page followers, or visitors. It does not read comments, messages, friend lists, or any user profile information.

How Information Is Used

  • To generate post captions (text prompts are sent to OpenAI's API; no personal data is included).
  • To publish scheduled posts to the Daily Wings & Cafe Facebook Page via the Meta Graph API.
  • Weather and public holiday context is fetched from Open-Meteo and Nager.Date; these requests contain no personal data.

Data Storage

Post drafts and publishing history are stored locally on our own server. Nothing is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties beyond the API services listed above, which are used only to operate the App.

Data Deletion

The App stores no personal data about Facebook users, so there is nothing to delete on request. If you believe the App holds any data relating to you, or you would like any content removed, contact us using the details below and we will respond promptly.
